Adaptation and Mitigation Initiative in Agriculture (AMIA) Program

DA’s banner program on climate change to  achieve its climate-resilient agriculture (CRA) agenda.

Addresses adaptation and mitigation in agriculture. 

Uses the AMIA Village Approach towards empowered and progressive communities in addressing the impacts of climate change for climate-resilient Philippine agriculture.

Provides information on the challenges posed by climate change.

Promotes Climate Resilient Agriculture (CRA) technologies and practices to empower communities to manage climate risks, while pursuing sustainable livelihoods; and

Delivers integrated and tailor-fitted support services from DA, LGUs, and other partners.
